In Lieu of Flowers:

    Laura Jane's son Thomas and his wife Erin are in the process of determining an appropriate way to honor Laura Jane's artistic legacy and memory. This may take the form of a modest materials scholarship or outreach program to help encourage UC Davis undergraduates who share Laura Jane's passions and talents, reprinted editions of her books whose proceeds might go towards an appropriate cause or simply a cumulative charitable donation to an institution that supports her creative values and love for Nature.

If you think you might have any future interest in helping us in this effort please consider signing up for the email list below. We will be in contact as soon as we have determined an appropriate and realistically achievable form of memorial.

If you happen to feel comfortable contributing any small or large amount before knowing the exact form the memorial fund will take we have also set up a contribution site through PayPal for the time being.

One of Laura Jane's greatest gifts to the world was the encouragement she gave others in their creative pursuits. For some of us, her love, support and inspirational artistic spirit have repeatedly been the defining factor in the decision to continue to commit our lives to the arts as she did. It is this love and encouragement that we hope to pass along in some small way to future young artists.

~ Thomas & Erin
